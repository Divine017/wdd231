About Our Chamber

Mission

Our mission is to support and promote local businesses, fostering a vibrant community through collaboration and innovation. We strive to create opportunities for growth, provide valuable resources, and build strong connections among businesses, ensuring a thriving and sustainable local economy for generations to come.

Vision

Our vision is to be the leading advocate for local businesses, creating a supportive environment that encourages innovation, collaboration, and growth. We aim to connect businesses with resources and opportunities that enhance their success and contribute to a thriving community.

Values

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of honesty and transparency in all our dealings.
  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of working together to achieve common goals.
  • Innovation: We embrace creativity and new ideas to drive progress and growth.
  • Community: We are committed to supporting and enhancing the local community.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in everything we do, delivering the best possible service to our members.
  • Inclusivity: We value diversity and inclusivity, ensuring that all voices are heard and respected.
  • Accountability: We take responsibility for our actions and their impact on the community.
  • Respect: We treat everyone with respect and dignity, fostering a positive and supportive environment.
  • Empowerment: We empower our members to succeed by providing the resources and support they need.
  • Leadership: We lead by example, inspiring others to take action and make a difference.
  • Adaptability: We are flexible and responsive to the changing needs of our members and the community.
  • Commitment: We are dedicated to the success of our members and the community as a whole.
  • Service: We are here to serve our members and the community, providing support and resources to help them thrive.
  • Trust: We build trust through open communication and reliable support.
  • Growth: We are committed to the growth and development of our members and the community.
  • Partnership: We believe in the power of partnerships to drive success and create opportunities.
  • Advocacy: We advocate for the interests of our members and the local business community.
  • Education: We provide educational resources and opportunities to help our members grow and succeed.
  • Networking: We facilitate networking opportunities to connect our members with each other and the community.
  • Support: We are here to support our members in their journey to success.
  • Community Engagement: We actively engage with the community to understand their needs and concerns.

Community Impact

Our Chamber is dedicated to making a positive impact in the local community through various initiatives and programs. We believe in giving back and supporting local causes that enhance the quality of life for all residents.

  • Launch the Timbuktu Youth Enterprise Program in 2013.
  • Providing scholarships and educational resources for local students.
  • Partnerned with 100+ businesses for local job creation.
  • Organized 40+ training and networking events annually.
